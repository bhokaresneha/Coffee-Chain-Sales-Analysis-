# ☕ Coffee Chain Sales Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes Coffee Chain Sales data using Python to uncover sales trends, product performance, profitability, market performance, and budget achievement. The goal is to generate actionable business insights that can help management make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Understand the structure and quality of the dataset
- Analyze overall sales and profit performance
- Identify top-performing products and product types
- Compare sales across markets and states
- Evaluate profitability and profit margins
- Analyze market size performance
- Compare actual performance against budget targets
- Generate meaningful business insights through data visualization

## 📊 Key Findings

### Sales Performance

- Total Sales: **$819,811**
- Total Profit: **$259,543**
- Average Sales per Transaction: **$193**
- Overall Profit Margin: **31.66%**

### Market Performance

- Highest Revenue Market: **West**
- Highest Transaction Market: **Central**
- Highest Revenue State: **California**

### Product Performance

- Most Profitable Product: **Columbian**
- Highest Revenue Product Type: **Espresso**
- Most Profitable Product Type: **Coffee**

### Budget Performance

- Sales Target Achievement: **109.87%**
- Sales exceeded budget by **$73,651**
- Profit Target Achieved
- 75.68% of transactions met or exceeded sales targets

### Profitability

- 86.82% of transactions were profitable
- Green Tea showed negative profitability and requires business review
